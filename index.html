Pet Walking

$12 per walk ($14.40 after web operations fee)

If you just need a quick walk, we will take your furry friend for a stroll while you're at work or busy with other activities! Our walks usually last between 30 minutes to an hour.

Pet Sitting

$35 per NIGHT ($42 after web operations fee)

For a longer term job, one of our experienced pet owners will stop by your home to feed, play with, and care for all of your pets while you're away on vacation! This service includes 2-3 walks and 2-3 feeds per day as well as any basic chores such as watering plants and collecting mail. If you'd like to talk before booking us, please text or email us!
