About Us

Maitland Pet & Babysitting is a family-owned business providing quality pet care and babysitting services to the Maitland area. We understand that your pets and children are a part of your family, and we treat them as if they were our own.

Our experienced and trustworthy team is dedicated to providing the best possible care for your pets and children, whether it's a quick pet walk or a long-term babysitting job. We are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

At Maitland Pet & Babysitting, we pride ourselves on our commitment to exceptional customer service. We work closely with our clients to ensure that we meet their needs and exceed their expectations. We believe that communication is key, and we keep our clients informed every step of the way.
