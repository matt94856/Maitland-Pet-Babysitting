About Us

Maitland's Best Pet Sitters is a family-owned business providing quality pet care services to the Maitland area. We understand that your pets are a part of your family, and we treat them as if they were our own.

Our experienced and trustworthy staff are all pet owners and we are dedicated to providing the best possible care for your pets, whether it's a quick pet walk or a long-term pet sitting job.

At Maitland's Best Pet Sitters, we pride ourselves on our commitment to exceptional customer service. We work closely with you to ensure that we meet your needs and exceed your expectations. We believe that communication is key, and we'll keep you informed every step of the way.
